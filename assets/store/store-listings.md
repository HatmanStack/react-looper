## Support Materials

### FAQ

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: No, Looper works completely offline after the initial download.

**Q: What audio formats are supported?**
A: Looper records and exports MP3 files. For import, most common audio formats are supported (MP3, WAV, M4A, etc.).

**Q: How many tracks can I mix?**
A: You can work with up to 20 tracks simultaneously.

**Q: Is there a time limit on recordings?**
A: Only limited by available storage on your device.

**Q: Does Looper send my audio to a server?**
A: No, all audio processing happens locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded.

**Q: Can I edit the audio (cut, trim, fade)?**
A: The current version focuses on mixing with speed and volume controls. Audio editing features may come in future updates.

### Troubleshooting

**Microphone not working?**

- Check that you've granted microphone permission
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your device's microphone settings

**Files won't import?**

- Check that the file is a supported audio format
- Ensure you have storage permission granted
- Try a different audio file

**Mixing is slow?**

- Mixing large files takes time
- Reduce the number of tracks
- Use shorter audio files for testing
